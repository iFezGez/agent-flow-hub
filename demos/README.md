# Demo Overview: Autonomous Lead Qualification

This demo showcases how an AI Agent acts as the first point of contact for incoming business inquiries.

### How it works:
1.  **Ingestion:** A lead submits a contact form (Webhook).
2.  **Analysis:** The AI Agent (GPT-4o) performs a semantic analysis of the query to identify intent, urgency, and potential budget.
3.  **Scoring:** Leads are scored from 1-10 based on business alignment.
4.  **Action:** 
    *   **High Score:** Immediate Slack/WhatsApp alert sent to the sales team.
    *   **Low Score:** Lead is added to a low-priority nurturing sequence in the CRM.

### Business Impact:
*   **Response Speed:** Initial analysis completed in < 5 seconds.
*   **Productivity:** Sales teams focus only on pre-qualified, high-intent leads.
*   **Consistency:** Every query is analyzed with the same professional criteria, 24/7.
